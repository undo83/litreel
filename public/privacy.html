LitReel ("we", "us") operates the website litreel.com, which features short video book trailers and book recommendations. The operator is Lamba Alexandru (an individual), based in Romania (EU). For any privacy question, contact contact@litreel.com.

We do not collect sensitive data and never ask for payment details — purchases happen on our partners' sites (Amazon, Bookshop.org), not on LitReel.

3. Why we process it & legal basis

  • Sending the newsletter — legal basis: your consent (GDPR art. 6(1)(a)). Unsubscribe anytime.
  • Running and securing the site — legal basis: our legitimate interest (art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Analytics & marketing (e.g. measuring ads) — legal basis: your consent via the cookie banner.

4. Who we share data with

We work with providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Google) — hosting, database (newsletter emails), infrastructure.
  • Affiliate partners — Amazon Associates and Bookshop.org. When you click a product link, an affiliate cookie lets a purchase be attributed to us. We do not share your email with them.
  • Advertising platforms (e.g. TikTok, Google) — only if you accept marketing cookies, to measure campaigns.

Some providers may process data outside the EU, under appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses).

5. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies and, with your consent, analytics and marketing cookies. Details in our Cookie Policy. You can change your choice anytime by clearing the site's cookies/storage and reloading.

6. How long we keep data

  • Newsletter email — until you unsubscribe or ask for deletion.
  • Technical/analytics data — limited periods per provider settings (typically up to 14-26 months).

7. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise them, email contact@litreel.com. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in Romania: ANSPDCP).
