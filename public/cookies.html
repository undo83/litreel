Cookie Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a site. They help the site work, remember preferences, and — with your consent — measure traffic and ad effectiveness.

Cookies we use

TypePurposeNeeds consent?
Strictly necessarySite functionality, remembering your cookie choiceNo
AnalyticsUnderstand how the site is used (pages, traffic sources)Yes
MarketingMeasure ad campaigns (e.g. TikTok Pixel, Google) and remarketingYes
AffiliateSet by partners (Amazon, Bookshop.org) to attribute a purchase from a clickOn click of the partner link

Affiliate cookies

When you click a link to a partner (Amazon or Bookshop.org), that partner may set a cookie so a commission can be attributed to us if you order. These cookies are controlled by the partner and their affiliate program, under their policies.

Managing your consent

On your first visit we show a banner where you can accept or reject analytics and marketing cookies. You can change your choice anytime: clear the site's cookies/storage in your browser and reload — the banner will reappear. You can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings.

More

How we handle your data is described in our Privacy Policy. Questions: contact@litreel.com.
